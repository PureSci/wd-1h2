    Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are some of the questions we often receive from our customers. Some may be added if you ask enough :)

    Can I teach my chicken tricks?

    Absolutely! Our chickens at The Chicken Market are known for their intelligence and friendly nature. While we can't guarantee they'll master advanced calculus, many of our chickens enjoy learning simple tricks like coming when called or hopping onto your lap. Just remember, patience and treats are key!

    What should I feed my new chicken friend?

    Your new chicken friend will thrive on a diet of high-quality chicken feed, fresh vegetables, and the occasional treat. Avoid feeding them chocolate, avocados, or anything toxic to birds. A happy chicken is a well-fed chicken, so ensure they have access to fresh water and a balanced diet to keep them clucking joyfully!

    Can my chicken sleep in my bed with me?

    While it might sound cozy to snuggle up with your feathered friend, it's best for both you and your chicken to have separate sleeping quarters. Chickens need a safe, comfortable space to roost at night, free from disturbances. Providing them with a proper coop or a designated sleeping area will ensure they get the rest they need to stay healthy and happy.
